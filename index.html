About Us

Nelover Event Services is a full-service event management company operating across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. We specialize in planning, organizing, and executing all types of events — from government and corporate gatherings to festivals, exhibitions, and private celebrations.

With a wide range of high-quality rental equipment including PVC tents, portable toilets, seating, fences, booths, and lighting towers, we ensure every event is delivered with precision, professionalism, and care. Our experienced team is dedicated to turning ideas into impactful, memorable experiences — no matter the scale, location, or challenge. From Riyadh to Jeddah, AlUla to Neom, we are your trusted partner in event success.
